One day, a man named John went to the bookstore to buy a book. He picked out the book he wanted and paid for it at the cashier. After that, he went to the cafe to drink coffee. At the cafe, he met a woman named Sarah. They talked about the book John had just bought and found that they had a lot in common. They exchanged phone numbers and promised to meet again.

A few days later, John called Sarah and asked her out on a date. They went to a fancy restaurant and enjoyed a delicious dinner. After dinner, they went to the park and walked under the stars. They talked about the past and their dreams for the future. They felt very comfortable with each other.

A few weeks later, John and Sarah became a couple. They spent a lot of time together and supported each other in everything. They even started planning their future together.

However, one day, John had to move to another city because of his job. Sarah felt very sad because she had to part with John. However, they promised to stay in touch and visit each other.
